
Three persons were arrested on Tuesday on charges of selling pirated audio and video CDs and also pornographic CDs in various regional languages, police said. 


 Acting on a tip-off, a police team raided the residences of the accused and confiscated 9330 pirated audio-video cds of various languages and 1100 pornographic audio-videos cds worth Rs 4.66 lakh, police said.

Police also seized three mobile phones, a colour television and a DVD player.
